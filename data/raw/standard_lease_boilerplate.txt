APARTMENT: Generic Student Housing
SOURCE: Standard Lease Agreement Boilerplate

1. RENT AND PAYMENTS: Tenant agrees to pay base rent in equal monthly installments. Rent is due on the 1st of each month. A late fee of $50.00 will be assessed if rent is not received by the 3rd of the month.

2. GUARANTOR REQUIREMENT: Because Tenant is a student and may not meet the minimum income requirements (3x the monthly rent), a qualified Guarantor (co-signer) is required. The Guarantor is jointly and severally liable for all financial obligations under this Lease.

3. SUBLEASING AND REASSIGNMENT: Tenant may not sublet the premises or assign this Lease without prior written consent from Management. An administrative fee of $250.00 applies to any approved lease takeover or reassignment.

4. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS: Management is responsible for routine maintenance. However, Tenant will be charged for any damages caused by negligence, including clogged plumbing due to improper disposal or drywall damage from unauthorized mounting of TVs or shelving.

5. QUIET HOURS: Quiet hours are strictly enforced between 10:00 PM and 7:00 AM. Repeated noise complaints may result in fines or eviction.
